About Us

SoftStudy is a digital platform where we aim to empower and impact the lives of many students regardless of their level of education and to overcome their study challenges and fears. A space where all students have the opportunity to learn and grow at their own pace with the aid of our study smarter features.

SoftStudy will help every student achieve the best they can.

Mission

Our mission is to help and impact students as possible through our digital platform regardless of their level of education to overcome their study challenges and fears. To also revolutionize the way students learn, so that every child is empowered to achieve their full potential.

Vision

Our vision is to adopt the use of data and technology through our digital platform to help students study better and overcom any challenges they face while studying, while making it easy and fun.

The Team

We are a group of young entrepreneurs who have faced study challenges and have made some decisions in regard to studying, so we decided to come together to proffer a solution by developing SoftStudy.
